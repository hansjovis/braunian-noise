What's in a Name?

The name of this site, Braunian Noise, is a wordplay on Brownian Noise, a type of noise that is characterized by more low than high frequencies. It sounds like a waterfall, or distant traffic and is generally regarded to be more pleasing to the ear than noise with a higher high frequency content (e.g. white noise).
